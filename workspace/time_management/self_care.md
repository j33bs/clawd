# Self-Care Suggestions

## Categories

### Movement & Body
- "15-minute stretch or yoga flow"
- "Quick walk around the block"
- "5 minutes of jumping jacks or bodyweight"
- "Stand and move for 2 minutes"
- "Shoulder rolls and neck stretches"

### Mental & Mind
- "5-minute meditation or breathing exercise"
- "Journal 3 things you're grateful for"
- "Write down what's on your mind"
- "Read 10 pages of a book"
- "Listen to one song you love"

### Rest & Recharge
- "10-minute power nap"
- "Close your eyes and breathe deeply"
- "Make a cup of tea - enjoy the process"
- "Step outside and look at the sky"
- "Do nothing for 5 minutes"

### Connection
- "Text someone you haven't talked to"
- "Send a gratitude message"
- "Call a friend or family member"
- "Share something you're working on"

### Environment
- "Clean your workspace quickly"
- "Organize one drawer or surface"
- "Open a window for fresh air"
- "Water a plant"
- "Light a candle or play music"

### Morning (before work)

### Midday
- 5-min stretch

- Music break

### Evening

- Journaling

- Creative play
