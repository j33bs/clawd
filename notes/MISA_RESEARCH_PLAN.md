# MISA Research Plan

## Objective
Conduct thorough research on MISA (Men's Information and Support Agency) to understand current services and avoid redundant suggestions in the development plan.

## Research Tasks

### 1. Organization Overview
- Mission and vision statement
- Current services offered
- Target demographics served
- Geographic coverage area
- Staff composition and qualifications
- Years of operation

### 2. Current Programs and Services
- Counseling and therapy services
- Support groups offered
- Crisis intervention services
- Community outreach programs
- Online resources and tools
- Specialized programs (veterans, indigenous, etc.)

### 3. Service Delivery Methods
- In-person services
- Online/telehealth offerings
- Group versus individual services
- Drop-in versus scheduled appointments
- Community partnership programs

### 4. Current Gaps and Challenges
- Identified service gaps
- Funding challenges
- Staffing issues
- Community needs unmet
- Geographic access limitations

### 5. Referral Networks
- Current referral partners
- Healthcare system connections
- Community service partnerships
- Crisis intervention links
- Specialist service connections

### 6. Funding Sources and Fundraising
- Current funding model
- Grant funding sources
- Corporate sponsors
- Individual donors
- Fundraising events and activities

### 7. Marketing and Outreach
- Current social media presence
- Marketing strategies
- Community engagement approaches
- Public awareness campaigns
- Brand messaging and positioning

## Research Methodology

### Phase 1: Digital Research (Week 1)
- Visit official MISA website
- Review social media accounts
- Search news articles and press releases
- Check government and community databases
- Review annual reports if available

### Phase 2: Stakeholder Input (Week 2)
- Interview current staff/volunteers
- Survey service users
- Consult with board members
- Reach out to partner organizations
- Gather feedback from community leaders

### Phase 3: Gap Analysis (Week 3)
- Compare current services with community needs
- Identify overlapping recommendations
- Highlight missed opportunities
- Assess resource allocation efficiency
- Evaluate service delivery effectiveness

## Deliverables
- Comprehensive MISA profile
- Service gap analysis report
- Competitive analysis with other men's services
- Updated recommendations avoiding redundancy
- Prioritized action plan based on actual needs

## Timeline
- Week 1: Complete digital research
- Week 2: Complete stakeholder interviews
- Week 3: Complete gap analysis
- Week 4: Deliver updated recommendations

## Resources Needed
- Contact information for MISA leadership
- Access to organizational documents
- Permission for stakeholder interviews
- Budget for research activities if required
- Time allocation for research team

This research plan will ensure that all future recommendations for MISA are based on current reality rather than assumptions, preventing redundancy and maximizing impact.
